Command Modern Operations Log Analysis Tool


(Work in Progress)

This is an initial draft that showcases some of the tool's capabilities. It allows for the extraction of data related to detected contacts, weapons, sensors, platforms, and more from game logs. However, please note that as it's still under development, there may be bugs, inaccuracies, and errors present. I'm continuously working on refining and improving the tool to provide more accurate and reliable results.

Contacts

This section presents a timeline displaying the number of newly detected contacts and classified contacts, grouped into 30-minute intervals, for each side. It provides a visual representation of the evolving situation over time. Furthermore, the analysis extends to examining the types of newly discovered contacts for each side. By studying this data, one can observe significant moments such as when the highest number of missiles is detected, be it against the friendly or enemy fleet, or when the anti-aircraft defenses become operational. These insights shed light on critical events and patterns, offering a deeper understanding of the dynamics and strategic developments throughout the timeline.

Weapon Guide Performance

The following charts depict the performance of weapons. A weapon's performance can result in five different outcomes: 'Impact' when the weapon successfully hits its target, 'Missed' when the weapon fails to hit the target due to factors such as running out of fuel, lack of guidance, or self-destruction, 'Malfunction' when the weapon experiences a technical failure, 'Spoofed' when the weapon is deceived by an enemy decoy and 'Jammed' when the weapon's functionality is disrupted by a DECM (Defensive Electronic Countermeasure). To provide greater clarity, the weapon guide is categorized into 'Air Missiles' and 'Land/Sea Missiles.' 'Air Missiles' encompass both air-to-air and surface-to-air missiles while 'Land/Sea Missiles' enclose air-to-ground and surface-to-surface missiles.
