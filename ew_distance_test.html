EW vs Distance

The test involved three distinct electronic warfare aircraft and three different radar systems. The aircraft were positioned at varying distances from the radar and flew in circular patterns, covering a range of 10 to 520 nautical miles in increments of 30 nautical miles. Due to the circular flight path, the actual distance between the aircraft and the radar could vary. A total of 57 iterations of the test were conducted, yielding the following results.

Radar (Flat Face E [P-19, Kasta 2E2 Upgrade])

Radar (Back Net [P-80])

Radar (Cheese Board [96L6])
