EW Test

In the following charts, I examine how three EW aircraft (EA-18G Growler, EC-37B Compass Call, and J-16D) impact the detection of an F-16A aircraft across different radar systems. The negative distance values represent situations where no EW aircraft is present. I conducted tests using CMO version 1328.5 and coarse simulation (flame speed), evaluating distances from 0 to 400 nautical miles at a 20-mile pace and from 0 to 800 miles at a 50-mile pace
